Panchgani hill station

Panchgani locally means ‘ the land of five hills’. Located approximately 100 kms from Pune, Panchgani is one of the best hill stations in Maharashtra, which allures visitors because of its pristine beauty, dense forests, and the best views of Sahyadri. The gushing Bhilar and Lingmala waterfalls amidst dense forests are worth a sight. How to reach: Nearest air connection and railhead is in Pune. If you want to enjoy the misty roads and picturesque scenes, simply drive to this place. Things to do: Cycling, local shopping and cave hiking at Rajpuri caves. People also go for sightseeing places like Table Land, Sydney Point, and Arthur Seat.
