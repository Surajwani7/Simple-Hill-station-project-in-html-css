Matheran hill station

With the backdrop of plush green Sahyadri hills, Matheran hill station is one of the most scenic and serene hill stations near Mumbai. It overjoys travellers with unpolluted and refreshing environment. This is the only eco-friendly hill station in Maharashtra, certified by Ministry of Environment and Forests and the only automobile-free hill station in India. How to reach: The closest airport is at Mumbai, whereas Neral, 20 kms from Matheran is the nearest railway station. Buses ply from Mumbai and Pune up to Neral, and from there one can reach the destination by the toy train. Things to do: Visit One Tree Hill Point, Echo Point, Alexander Point, and Prabal Fort. Go for trekking, valley crossing, and nature walking. .
