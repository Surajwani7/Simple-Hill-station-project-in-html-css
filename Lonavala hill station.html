Lonavala hill station

Among the most celebrated hill stations near Mumbai and Pune, Lonavala hill station is a trekker’s paradise. With many waterfalls, caves, trekking and hiking points, it has become a favourite destination of adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers. And being a part of Sahyadri mountains, it offers some magnificent vistas and How to reach: Lohegaon Airport in Pune is the nearest airport and Lonavala has its own railhead. People also prefer to drive from Mumbai and Pune. Ideal duration: 1-2 days Things to do: Explore Rajmachi Wildlife Sanctuary, Amrutanjan Point and Bhaja Caves. Trek up to Koregad Fort, Tiger’s Leap, Duke’s Nose, and Kondane Caves. You can also go for boating in Lonavala Lake.
